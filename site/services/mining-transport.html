Mining Transport

Specialised Heavy-Duty Transport for South Africa’s Harshest Environments

In mining, every delivery matters because every delay costs. At BAC Logistics, we understand the complexities of the mining sector and deliver precision-driven, regulation-compliant, and terrain-ready transport services that meet the industry’s exacting standards.

From explosives and heavy machinery to hazardous materials and sensitive electronics, we provide the vehicles, protocols, and people required to move your cargo securely and efficiently, even to the most remote or high-risk mining sites across Southern Africa.

 

Built for the Mining Industry

Mining logistics is not general logistics. It involves:

- Transporting abnormal loads and equipment under strict timeframes
- Managing hazardous materials with zero tolerance for error
- Navigating extreme terrain, weight restrictions, and on-site clearance
- Complying with mine access rules, safety audits, and documentation
- Coordinating with multiple stakeholders: suppliers, engineers, mine managers

BAC Logistics is equipped and experienced to handle this. With over 25 years in the logistics industry, a fleet designed for harsh operating environments, and Dangerous Goods (DG) certified drivers, we are a trusted name in mining transport across South Africa and the SADC region.

Our Mining Transport Capabilities

Heavy Equipment & Machinery Transport

We provide road freight solutions for:

- Excavators, dump trucks, bulldozers, loaders, and graders
- Drilling rigs, compressors, crushers, and conveyor components
- Spare parts, crawler tracks, steel plates, and generators
- Plant components and structural elements (steel beams, tanks, supports)

We manage:

- Route planning for abnormal loads
- Escort vehicles and traffic permits
- Height, weight, and axle distribution assessments
- Load securing using certified ratchet straps and custom brackets

Dangerous Goods & Hazardous Materials

BAC is certified and experienced in the transport of hazardous goods commonly used in mining, including:

- Explosives (subject to licensing and SAPS approval)
- Lubricants, hydraulic fluids, and fuel additives
- Acids, chemical reagents, and compressed gases

We ensure:

- Full Dangerous Goods documentation and SDS handling
- Vehicle compliance with hazardous load regulations
- Driver certification under national DG transport guidelines
- Route approvals and risk mitigation planning

Remote & High-Security Site Deliveries

Mining doesn’t happen in the city, and we’re equipped for that.

We deliver to:

- Remote operations in Northern Cape, Limpopo, North West, and Mpumalanga
- Cross-border mines in Botswana, Zambia, Namibia, and Zimbabwe
- Deep rural, off-grid locations requiring 4x4 access or convoy support
- High-security sites with restricted access, clearance protocols, and PPE enforcement

Our team coordinates directly with:

- Mine safety officers
- Security and gate access control
- Procurement teams and site engineers
- Local authorities (if required)

Compliance-First Transport Execution

At BAC Logistics, we don’t just move mining cargo, we move it by the book. We uphold strict internal standards and comply with all applicable mining, transport, and safety legislation.

Our compliance practices include:

- Mine Health & Safety Act (MHSA) adherence
- Daily driver briefings on site rules and cargo specifics
- Mandatory PPE for all drivers and assistants on mine property
- Vehicle and load inspections before dispatch
- Hazardous materials labelling and cargo signage
- Load securement audits for oversized or high-risk freight
- Compliance with transport permits, weight restrictions, and escorts for abnormal loads

All documentation, from consignment notes to delivery dockets, is signed off by receiving parties on-site to maintain clear chain-of-custody.

Fleet Capabilities & Technology

We deploy the right vehicle for the job, with fleet options that include:

- Flatbeds and drop-decks for machinery
- Superlinks and tri-axle trailers for volume loads
- Lowbeds for oversized or heavy cargo
- Curtain siders for weather-sensitive materials
- Crane trucks and mechanical offload vehicles (upon request)
- 4x4 support for last-mile access to difficult sites

All vehicles are equipped with:

- Mix Telematics tracking for real-time visibility
- Panic buttons, driver monitoring, and route alerts
- Immobilisation tech for high-value or high-risk loads
- Tamper-evident seals and lockable compartments (where required)

 

Who We Serve

Our mining clients include:

- Coal, gold, copper, and platinum operations
- Mid-sized and junior mines in ramp-up phases
- International exploration companies
- Drilling and blasting contractors
- Heavy equipment leasing companies
- OEMs and maintenance providers for mining equipment

Whether it’s a routine shipment of lubricants or a once-off move of an excavator, we treat each consignment with care, urgency, and compliance.
