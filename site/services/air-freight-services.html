Air Freight Services

Efficient, Reliable, and Global Air Freight Solutions

At BAC Logistics, we understand that time-sensitive shipments require speed, precision, and reliability. Our Air Freight Services are designed to provide end-to-end solutions for clients, offering both domestic and international coverage. With a global footprint, we specialise in air freight consolidations, cargo wholesale, and brokerage, ensuring your goods reach their destination swiftly and safely, no matter where in the world they need to go. 

Whether you're shipping locally or across continents, our team is equipped to handle it all, adhering to IATA regulations and providing expert management of even the most complex cargo.

Our Process

Consultation

We begin by understanding your specific shipping needs, including the nature of your cargo, destination, and any special requirements.

Planning & Documentation

Our team handles all necessary documentation and plans the most efficient route, considering factors like cost, speed, and security.

Cargo Handling

Whether it's General Cargo or Dangerous Goods, we ensure compliance with international standards and arrange for safe and secure transport.

Customs Clearance

Our certified CargoWise professionals manage the Customs process, ensuring smooth and timely clearance.

Delivery & Tracking

Throughout the journey, you’ll have access to real-time updates, and our team coordinates final delivery to ensure your cargo arrives as expected.
