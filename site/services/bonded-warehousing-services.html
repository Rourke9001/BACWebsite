Bonded Warehousing Services

Secure, Compliant, and Efficient Bonded Warehousing Solutions

BAC Logistics offers comprehensive Bonded Warehousing Services designed to facilitate the storage and management of duty-free goods. Our warehousing solutions not only ensure compliance with customs regulations but also provide flexibility for importers and exporters looking to store goods under customs control. 

With a global reach and extensive experience, we provide bonded warehousing services that cater to both local and international clients, ensuring secure storage and efficient customs handling to streamline your supply chain.

Our Process

Customs Consultation & Planning

Our team begins by assessing your storage requirements and ensuring full compliance with customs and excise regulations.

This includes understanding the nature of your goods, the expected storage period, import or export requirements, duty implications, and the customs processes needed to keep your cargo properly controlled while in bond.

Inventory Management

We utilise advanced tracking systems to monitor inventory, maintain bond registers, and provide detailed stock-on-hand audits, including stock duty schedules and expiry lists.

This gives importers, exporters, and supply chain teams better visibility over bonded stock, helping reduce discrepancies, improve reporting accuracy, and support smoother movement when goods are ready for release, transfer, or export.

Customs & Excise Audits

We facilitate and manage all customs audits, ensuring that all goods comply with applicable regulations while minimising potential delays.

Our team assists with the accurate records, supporting documentation, and stock information needed during customs and excise checks, helping clients maintain compliance and reduce unnecessary operational disruption.

Handling Imports and Exports

We manage all aspects of bonded imports and exports, including duty-free and bonded transfers, with strict adherence to global standards.

Whether goods are entering the country, moving under customs control, being transferred between facilities, or prepared for export, BAC Logistics helps coordinate the process so that documentation, handling, and compliance remain aligned.

End-to-End Security

With comprehensive security measures in place, we ensure that your goods are stored safely and remain compliant throughout their stay in our bonded facilities.

Our bonded warehousing solutions are designed to protect cargo integrity, support customs control, and give clients confidence that goods are managed securely from receipt to release.
