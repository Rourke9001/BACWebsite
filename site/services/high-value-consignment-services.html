Our Process

Initial Consultation

We begin with a thorough consultation to understand the nature of your high-value goods, specific requirements, and security needs.

This includes reviewing the value, sensitivity, destination, handling requirements, risk profile, documentation needs, and any confidentiality considerations linked to the consignment.

Customised Transport Plan

We create a tailored transport plan that considers optimal routes, security measures, and any regulatory compliance specific to the consignment.

Each movement is planned around the cargo’s specific requirements, including timing, route control, collection and delivery coordination, secure handover points, and the level of visibility required throughout the journey.

Enhanced Security Protocols

Our vehicles are equipped with advanced tracking systems, allowing for real-time monitoring and secure handling throughout transit.

Security measures are built into the movement from the start, helping protect valuable cargo through monitored transit, controlled communication, careful handling, and reduced exposure to unnecessary risk.

Customs Clearance & Compliance

Our team of experts manages all necessary documentation and customs clearance, ensuring seamless cross-border movements with full regulatory compliance. We also facilitate pre-clearance where possible.

For international and cross-border consignments, BAC Logistics helps ensure that documentation, customs requirements, and compliance procedures are aligned before the cargo moves, reducing the risk of clearance delays or avoidable disruption.

Final Delivery & Confirmation

We coordinate the final delivery with precision, confirming receipt and providing comprehensive updates at each stage of the journey.

The final handover is managed with the same level of care as the collection and transit process, ensuring the consignment reaches the correct destination securely and with clear delivery confirmation.
