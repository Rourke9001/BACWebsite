Sea Freight Services

Cost-effective and Reliable Sea Freight Solutions with Global Reach

BAC Logistics offers comprehensive Sea Freight Services designed to meet the needs of businesses looking to transport goods efficiently across the globe. With a global network and extensive partnerships, we provide a variety of sea freight options to suit your cargo requirements, from Full Container Loads (FCL) and Less than Container Loads (LCL) to project cargo and hazardous materials. 

Our team is well-versed in the intricacies of sea freight logistics, ensuring compliance with international regulations and delivering your goods safely and on time to over 400 destinations worldwide.

Our Process

Consultation & Planning

We begin by assessing your shipping requirements, considering factors like cargo type, destination, and budget.

Route Optimisation

Using our extensive network, we identify the most cost-effective and timely routes for your shipment.

Documentation & Compliance

We handle all necessary paperwork, from bills of lading to customs documentation, ensuring compliance with international maritime regulations.

Cargo Management

Whether your shipment is standard, oversized, or classified as Dangerous Goods, our team ensures that it’s loaded, stowed, and shipped with the highest level of care and security.

Customs Clearance & Delivery

We oversee customs clearance at all stages, coordinating with local authorities to expedite the process and ensure timely delivery to the final destination.
