Our Customs Clearing Services

Full-Service Import and Export Declarations

We handle customs declarations for all modes of freight, whether you’re moving goods by:

- Air freight (including AOG and time-critical cargo)
- Sea freight (FCL, LCL, RORO, Break Bulk)
- Road freight (local and cross-border transport)

We prepare and submit all necessary documentation to SARS and regional authorities, using electronic data interchange (EDI) for faster processing and reduced errors.

HS Code Classification & Tariff Interpretation

Misclassified goods can result in incorrect duties, rejected shipments, and non-compliance penalties. Our team ensures your cargo is assigned the correct Harmonised System (HS) codes, including tariff headings and duty rates.

We also assist in:

- Understanding preferential duty structures (SADC, EU)
- Applying for rebates or exemptions where applicable
- Validating whether your cargo qualifies for any trade agreement benefits

VAT, Duties & Rebates Handling

We calculate and assist with:

- Import VAT and customs duties
- Customs Value adjustments
- Rebate claims and refund processes
- Deferred VAT accounting for qualified importers

Our team ensures you never pay more than necessary and helps unlock cash flow advantages wherever possible.

Dangerous Goods & Specialised Cargo Clearance

BAC is certified to handle Dangerous Goods (DG), including:

- Chemicals and lubricants
- Explosives and mining-related cargo
- Medical and pharmaceutical DG
- Batteries and electronics with special declarations

We also offer clearance for:

- High-value and bonded items
- Oversized cargo
- Fragile and temperature-sensitive goods
- Temporary imports (for repairs, demos, or exhibitions)

Bonded Cargo & Road Bond Processing

For shipments moving under bonded status, such as cargo not yet customs-cleared or destined for a bonded warehouse, we provide:

- Road bond authorisations for SADC cross-border transport
- Integration with bonded warehousing solutions
- Security-sealed and tracked transport compliance
- In-transit documentation and monitoring

This is especially valuable for businesses using deferred payment strategies or storing goods duty-suspended until sale.

Port, Airport & Border Coordination

We manage relationships with customs authorities and third parties, including:

- Port terminals and depots (Durban, Cape Town, Gqeberha, Maputo, etc.)
- O.R. Tambo International and other airports
- Border posts (Beitbridge, Lebombo, Skilpadshek, and more)
- Bonded warehouse facilities across Southern Africa

Our team coordinates inspections, releases, and collections, ensuring on-time delivery from entry to final destination.

Industries We Serve

BAC Logistics has deep customs experience in the following sectors:

- Manufacturing: Raw materials, components, capital machinery
- Retail & FMCG: Consumer electronics, fast-moving goods, e-commerce
- Automotive: Parts, lubricants, diagnostic tools, bonded warehouse goods
- Mining & Industrial: Explosives, DGs, large equipment, project cargo
- Luxury & High-Value: Duty-free items, watches, jewellery, premium electronics

Every industry has its own documentation, restrictions, and security needs, and we handle each one with confidence and expertise.
