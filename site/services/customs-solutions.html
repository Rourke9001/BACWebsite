Our Customs Services

Customs Clearing for Businesses

Our clearing services ensure your cargo moves smoothly through customs with no hidden surprises. Whether you’re shipping raw materials, finished goods, or bonded inventory, our experts handle the documentation, classification, and clearance process from end to end.

Core offerings:

- Import and export declarations (air, sea, road)
- HS code classification and tariff consulting
- VAT, duties, and rebate administration
- Bonded cargo handling and road bond processing
- Dangerous goods (DG) and restricted items compliance
- Port, border post, and bonded warehouse integration

Ideal for:

Manufacturers, importers/exporters, logistics teams, freight forwarders, bonded goods managers

Specialised Customs Consultations

Not every business has the internal expertise to manage customs complexities, and that’s where we come in. Our consultation services give your business access to decades of regulatory knowledge, helping you remain compliant while unlocking cost savings.

Consultation services include:

- Trade compliance audits and risk assessments
- HS code reviews and updates
- SARS documentation guidance and dispute support
- Trade agreement utilisation (SADC, SACU, EU)
- Temporary Import Permit (TIP) advisory
- Rebates, refunds, and permit planning
- Assistance with Certificates of Origin and Destination

Ideal for:

Businesses expanding into new markets, undergoing audits, or requiring long-term customs planning and strategy.

Pre-Clearance for Businesses & Individuals

For urgent, high-value, or sensitive shipments, pre-clearance ensures your goods are cleared with customs before they arrive. This is especially useful for businesses handling time-critical inventory and for individuals importing luxury items or specialised personal effects.

Pre-clearance benefits:

- Shortened border and port processing times
- Avoidance of demurrage and warehousing fees
- Proactive compliance and faster delivery schedules
- Advanced issue resolution before goods land
- Confidential handling for sensitive or private shipments

Common use cases:

- Aircraft parts (AOG cargo)
- Luxury goods (jewellery, watches, fashion)
- Art, firearms, and collectables
- Promotional items and event materials
- Urgent medical, pharmaceutical, or DG cargo

Ideal for:

High-net-worth individuals, VIP travellers, retailers, corporate procurement teams, and air cargo handlers.
