Clear Before You Arrive. Move Without Delay.

How Our Pre-Clearance Process Works

1. Pre-Arrival Consultation

We start by understanding your shipment details. 

Including:

- Nature and value of goods
- Country of origin
- Freight method (air, sea, road)
- Intended destination
- Required permits or security measures

This allows us to map out the best approach, whether standard clearance, bonded routing, or temporary import frameworks.

2. Advance Document Submission

We prepare and file all necessary documentation before the cargo departs or while it’s in transit.

This may include:

- Commercial invoices
- Packing lists
- HS code classification
- Certificates of Origin or Destination
- Permits or licences (e.g. SAPS, NRCS)
- Waybills or transport documents
- Temporary Import applications (if applicable)

Our CargoWise system integrates with SARS and regional authorities, ensuring fast, paperless submission.

3. Proactive Compliance Review

We engage directly with customs officials to review:

- Duty and VAT assessments
- Risk indicators
- Inspection requirements
- Licensing or security flags

If corrections or clarifications are needed, we address them before the shipment lands, avoiding last-minute delays.

4. Arrival & Final Clearance

Once your shipment arrives:

- Inspection (if triggered) is scheduled and managed
- Cargo is released immediately if pre-cleared
- Transport to the bonded warehouse or final delivery is arranged
- Confirmation and full audit trail are provided

Use Cases: When Pre-Clearance Adds Real Value

AOG and Aircraft Spares

Aircraft on Ground (AOG) scenarios require immediate parts delivery. Pre-clearance allows critical cargo like engines, landing gear, or avionics to move from the airport to the hangar without delay.

Jewellery, Luxury Goods & Collectables

Private individuals or retailers bringing in high-value goods benefit from:
- Confidential handling
- Early VAT/duty calculation
- Secure release into bonded or private storage
- SAPS & SARS permit alignment (for watches, jewellery, etc.)

Event or Exhibition Logistics

Whether launching a product or attending a global trade show, timing is everything. We ensure marketing materials, display equipment, and promo stock are:

- Pre-cleared
- Delivered on schedule
- Returned via Temporary Import Permit if applicable

High-Volume Imports

Retailers or manufacturers importing seasonal or high-volume cargo can reduce warehousing costs and speed up last-mile delivery by having customs clearance done before stock arrives.
