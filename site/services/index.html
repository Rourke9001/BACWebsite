Specialised Logistics Solutions

At BAC Logistics, we provide reliable, compliant and end-to-end logistics solutions for businesses across South Africa, the SADC region and global trade routes. From road, air and sea freight to customs clearing, bonded warehousing and specialist cargo handling, our team ensures your goods move safely, efficiently and with full regulatory support every step of the way.

Road Freight Services

Reliable local and cross-border transport across South Africa and the SADC region, with tracked, compliant delivery.

Cross-Border & Local Transport

Reliable local and SADC transport solutions with road bond capability, customs support, real-time tracking, and secure delivery.

Mining Transport

Heavy-duty, compliant transport for mining equipment, dangerous goods, spares, and remote site deliveries.

Liquor Transport Services

Licensed, secure beverage transport with documentation, bonded integration, temperature control, and full compliance.

Customs Solutions

End-to-end customs support for air, sea, and road freight, helping cargo clear faster and compliantly.

Customs Clearing for Businesses

Fast, compliant import and export clearance across air, sea, and road freight, with expert documentation and duty support.

Customs Consultations

Strategic customs guidance for HS codes, rebates, trade agreements, audits, permits, and long-term compliance planning.

Pre-Clearance Services for Businesses & Individuals

Advance customs clearance for urgent, high-value, or sensitive shipments, reducing delays before your cargo arrives.

Air Freight Services

Fast, compliant air cargo solutions for time-critical shipments, dangerous goods, consolidations, and global delivery.

Sea Freight Services

Cost-effective FCL, LCL, RORO, break bulk, and project cargo solutions to over 400 global destinations.

Bonded Warehousing Services

Secure duty-suspended storage with customs compliance, stock audits, bond administration, and duty-free expertise.

High-Value Consignment Services

Discreet, secure handling for valuable and sensitive cargo, backed by tracking, compliance, and specialist care.

Aerospace & AOG Cargo

Urgent, specialist logistics for aircraft parts, engines, and AOG shipments where every minute matters.
