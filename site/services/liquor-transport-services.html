Liquor Transport Services

Licensed, Secure, and Compliant Beverage Logistics Across South Africa and the SADC Region

The transportation of alcohol is not just about moving bottles from point A to B, it’s about protecting your brand, complying with strict provincial laws, and preserving product quality from the moment it leaves the cellar to the moment it reaches the retailer or export market.

At BAC Logistics, we offer specialised Liquor Transport Services that are fully compliant, secure, and designed for the complex nature of alcohol logistics. With decades of experience and licensed transport authority across all provinces, BAC is the trusted logistics partner for wineries, beverage distributors, importers, and duty-free operators throughout Southern Africa.

 

End-to-End Liquor Logistics

South Africa’s liquor industry is tightly regulated. Transporters must comply with a range of provincial licensing laws, duty-free requirements, bonded warehouse conditions, and customs regulations when moving alcohol, especially for resale, export, or bonded storage.

BAC Logistics ensures that every shipment is:

- Properly licensed and documented
- Securely transported and protected from tampering or theft
- Temperature-controlled where needed
- Fully traceable with proof of delivery at every handover point
- Integrated with bonded warehousing or duty-free systems as required

Our services are designed to reduce risk, maintain product quality, and simplify compliance, so you can focus on selling, not stressing over logistics.

What We Offer

Licensed Liquor Transport

We are officially licensed to transport alcoholic beverages across all nine provinces in South Africa and are familiar with local licensing requirements, permit renewals, and interprovincial restrictions.

- Legal transport of wine, spirits, beer, RTDs, and fortified products
- Cross-provincial and cross-border liquor movement
- Driver briefings on compliance, load handling, and route restrictions
- Assistance with liquor transport documentation, where needed

Whether you're distributing from Stellenbosch to Sandton, or exporting from Cape Town to Windhoek, we make sure your cargo moves legally and securely.

Temperature-Controlled Transport

Alcohol is sensitive to temperature fluctuations. Prolonged exposure to heat or cold can degrade product quality, damage labels, or compromise packaging.

BAC provides:

- Temperature-controlled vehicles for premium wines, craft beer, and spirits
- Real-time temperature monitoring systems
- Insulated containers for long-haul journeys
- Seasonal route planning to avoid climate exposure

This is especially critical for:

- Boutique and estate wines
- Artisan gins and craft liquors
- High-value vintage or collectible alcohol
- Alcohol destined for export or long-term bonded storage

Secure and Tracked Delivery

We take liquor security seriously. Our systems are designed to protect your cargo from theft, tampering, and in-transit damage.

Features include:

- Mix Telematics GPS tracking on all vehicles
- POD (Proof of Delivery) systems with time-stamped confirmations
- Tamper-evident seals and security strapping
- Alert systems for route deviations or vehicle stoppages
- Driver behaviour monitoring and restricted route protocols for high-risk areas

We provide clear chain-of-custody documentation and sign-offs at every point of transfer, giving you total visibility and control.

Bonded Warehouse Integration

For clients dealing with duty-free or export-bound liquor, BAC offers seamless integration with our bonded warehousing and customs clearing services.

- Direct delivery to and from bonded warehouses
- Road bond transport of uncleared alcohol to export hubs
- Customs pre-clearance for cross-border movement
- Temporary storage options for in-transit consignments
- Coordination with airport duty-free operators and export terminals

This makes BAC a one-stop solution for brands managing international logistics or distributing via bonded channels.

Who We Serve

Wine Estates & Cellars

We transport bottled stock and bulk wine from cellar door to retailer, bonded warehouse, or port, with full temperature and security control.

Importers & Exporters

BAC handles customs documentation, bonded movement, and route-optimised delivery for imported or export-grade alcoholic goods.

Duty-Free Retailers & Distributors

Our team coordinates directly with duty-free stores, bonded warehouses, and customs officials to ensure regulatory compliance for in-transit liquor.

Beverage Wholesalers & National Distributors

For clients managing multi-brand stock across provinces or into neighbouring countries, we offer reliable FTL (Full Truck Load) and LTL (Less Than Truck Load) options, with flexible dispatch schedules and multi-drop capabilities.
