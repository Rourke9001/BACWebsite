Our Consultation Services

HS Code Classification & Tariff Review

Correct HS code classification is the cornerstone of compliant trade. An incorrect code can result in overpayment of duties, delays, or even seizure of goods. We assist with:

- Reviewing and validating product classifications
- Analysing tariff headings and subheadings
- Advising on applicable duty rates
- Classifying Dangerous Goods and controlled items

We also help clients explore tariff code alternatives that may qualify for lower duty rates or exemptions under trade agreements.

Trade Agreement Utilisation

Are you fully leveraging South Africa’s trade agreements? Our consultants help businesses benefit from preferential duty rates available through:

- SADC (Southern African Development Community)
- SACU (Southern African Customs Union)
- European Union (EU)
- UK Trade Partnership
- African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA)

We assist with:

- Eligibility assessments
- Origin declarations
- Certificate of Origin applications
- Trade documentation reviews

This helps reduce landed costs and improve profit margins, especially for manufacturers and exporters.

Rebates, Refunds & VAT Recovery

Many businesses miss out on valuable rebates and duty recovery opportunities.

We help you reclaim what you're entitled to, including:

- Rebate code guidance and eligibility reviews
- Application and compliance support
- VAT recovery on exported or re-exported goods
- Advice on deferred VAT and bonded inventory

Our approach ensures that you're not leaving money on the table and that your rebate strategies align with SARS requirements.

Temporary Import Permits (TIPs) & Special Entry Procedures

Need to bring items into South Africa temporarily for repair, demonstration, or exhibition?

We offer guidance on:

- TIP applications
- Security bond structuring
- Temporary admission documentation
- Monitoring and compliance to avoid penalties on overstays or missed re-exports

TIPs are particularly valuable for:

- Aircraft spares (e.g., AOG)
- Exhibition or demo equipment
- Commercial goods pending resale
- Personal effects for relocating individuals

Regulatory Risk Audits & Compliance Checks

Want to future-proof your operations? We offer internal audits to help you identify and correct compliance risks before they become costly.

 This includes:

- SARS customs compliance reviews
- Documentation and declaration audits
- Duty and VAT reconciliation
- Training and upskilling for internal logistics teams

These services are ideal for businesses preparing for expansion, applying for AEO status, or working toward trade certification standards.
