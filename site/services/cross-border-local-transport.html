Our Cross-Border & Local Transport Services

Full Truckload (FTL) and Less Than Truckload (LTL)

Whether you need an exclusive truck for high-volume shipments or a cost-effective shared load, we provide flexible options to suit your cargo needs.

- FTL: Best for bulk shipments, high-value cargo, or dedicated delivery timelines
- LTL: Ideal for smaller consignments, shared space, and optimised transport costs
- Consolidation and deconsolidation services available for regional hubs
- Guaranteed load security and cargo segregation to prevent contamination

General & Hazardous Cargo Transport

We transport a wide range of freight, including:

- General dry goods and palletised freight
- Chemicals and industrial materials
- Mining-related cargo and DG items

All BAC drivers are Dangerous Goods (DG) certified, and we maintain strict protocols for DG handling, documentation, and road signage.

Road Bond for Bonded Cargo

BAC holds road bonds to authorise and allow for the movement of bonded cargo across South African and SADC borders.

This allows us to:

- Transport goods that are in bond 
- Move bonded cargo between bonded warehouses
- Reduce client duty exposure until final clearance
- Enable regional inventory centralisation or consolidation

Our bonded cargo process includes:

- Customs-compliant vehicle sealing
- Real-time road bond tracking
- Full traceability of chain-of-custody at all checkpoints
- Clearance support at origin and destination

Real-Time Vehicle Tracking & Route Optimisation

All BAC vehicles are equipped with Mix Telematics GPS tracking, offering:

- Live vehicle tracking and location updates
- Route optimisation to reduce delivery times and fuel costs
- Geo-fencing and exception alerts
- Driver behaviour analytics for safety and accountability

Clients have access to real-time visibility and proactive updates, allowing logistics teams to plan better, reduce disruptions, and maintain accurate delivery schedules.

Customs Documentation & Border Clearance

We simplify the cross-border clearance process by handling all documentation and regulatory requirements in-house.

Services include:

- Import/export declarations and pre-clearance
- HS code verification and tariff alignment
- SADC Certificate of Origin preparation
- Waybills, commercial invoices, and packing lists
- Dangerous Goods permits and compliance
- Assistance with customs inspections and border post formalities

As a SARS AEO-accredited Level 2 and CargoWise-certified team, we operate with recognised international compliance standards, ensuring faster border clearance and fewer inspections.
