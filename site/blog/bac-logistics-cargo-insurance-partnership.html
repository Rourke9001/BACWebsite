Cargo Insurance Now Offered by BAC Logistics

2 April 2026

Tags: BAC Logistics insured cargo transport partnership with Mont Blanc

Your Cargo, Covered with Confidence

When moving cargo across borders, whether by air, sea, or along vital road networks, peace of mind is as important as precision. At BAC Logistics, we understand that every consignment represents more than goods; it represents trust, timelines, and, in many cases, millions of rands' worth of investment.

That’s why we’ve partnered with Mont Blanc Financial Services (MBFS), a trusted insurance broker, to bring our clients world-class insurance solutions across every service we offer.

 

Why Insurance Matters in Global Logistics

Global supply chains face risks every day: weather disruptions, port delays, theft, accidents, and even unforeseen geopolitical events. While our team works tirelessly to mitigate risks through operational excellence, true peace of mind comes from knowing that if the unexpected happens, your cargo remains protected.

By securing seamless insurance cover for every shipment, BAC ensures that your goods, whether they are aircraft engines, mining equipment, liquor consignments, or cross-border retail goods, are backed by a reliable safety net.

 

How the Cover Works

Through our partnership with MBFS, your cargo benefits from a multi-layered insurance structure that combines local expertise with global strength:

  • Mont Blanc Financial Services (MBFS) – Insurance Broker

  • TTM Risk (Pty) Ltd – Underwriting Manager

  • New National Assurance Company (Pty) Ltd – Insurance Provider

  • Munich Re – Global Reinsurance Partner

This means your shipments are not only covered by a reputable local provider but also supported by Munich Re, one of the world’s leading reinsurers. The result: a robust, reliable framework designed for dependability.

 

Insurance Across All BAC Services

The MBFS partnership is not limited to one division; it covers the full scope of BAC’s logistics expertise:

  • Air Freight – High-value, urgent shipments delivered with speed and security.

  • Sea Freight – Large-scale consignments are protected across international waters.

  • Road Freight – From cross-border and local transport to specialised mining and liquor consignments, every route is covered.

  • Bonded Warehousing – Protection while your goods are securely stored in customs-controlled facilities.

  • High-Value Consignments – From luxury goods to critical machinery, shipments are safeguarded every step of the way.

  • Customs Clearing, Consultations & Pre-Clearance – Insurance seamlessly integrates into our clearing services.

  • Aerospace & AOG Cargo – Precision logistics for urgent aerospace consignments, backed by the confidence of comprehensive cover.

 

Delivering Confidence Beyond Borders

At BAC Logistics, we believe logistics is more than moving goods from point A to point B. It’s about building trust, securing timelines, and delivering confidence across borders.

With our insurance partnership, your cargo is more than transported; it’s protected. That’s the BAC promise.
