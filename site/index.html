Welcome to BAC Logistics

Your Trusted Partner in End-to-End Logistics and Transportation Solutions

At BAC Logistics, we believe that every journey is unique, and so are the logistics solutions required to make it seamless. With over 25 years in the industry, we are proud to be a leading provider of integrated logistics, transport, and retail services across South Africa and beyond. From the moment your goods leave their origin to the time they reach their destination, we manage every step with precision, efficiency, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Who We Are

Founded by industry veterans Broughton and Julie Amiss, BAC Logistics has evolved from a successful duty-free retail background at O R Tambo International Airport into a full-service logistics company. Today, we are trusted by a diverse range of clients for our reliable and tailored solutions, providing everything from bonded warehousing to customs clearing, and cross-border transport.

As a member of the Diamond Gem & Jewellery Association of Southern Africa (DGJASA), internationally recognised JC Trans, and with Customs AEO accreditation, we adhere to the highest standards in the industry. Additionally, our team consists of certified CargoWise professionals, ensuring that our clients benefit from cutting-edge technology and compliance expertise.

Our Services

Air Freight

Fast, compliant air cargo solutions for time-critical shipments, dangerous goods, consolidations, and global delivery.

Sea Freight

Cost-effective FCL, LCL, RORO, break bulk, and project cargo solutions to over 400 global destinations.

Bonded Warehousing

Secure duty-suspended storage with customs compliance, stock audits, bond administration, and duty-free expertise.

High-Value Consignments

Discreet, secure handling for valuable and sensitive cargo, backed by tracking, compliance, and specialist care.

Road Freight

Reliable local and cross-border transport across South Africa and the SADC region, with tracked, compliant delivery.

Customs Clearing

End-to-end customs support for air, sea, and road freight, helping cargo clear faster and compliantly.

Aerospace & AOG Cargo

Urgent, specialist logistics for aircraft parts, engines, and AOG shipments where every minute matters.

Mining Logistics

Heavy-duty, compliant transport for mining equipment, dangerous goods, spares, and remote site deliveries.

Liquor Logistics

Licensed, secure beverage transport with documentation, bonded integration, temperature control, and full compliance.
