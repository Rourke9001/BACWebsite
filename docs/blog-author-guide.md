# Publishing a blog post — BAC Logistics

Audience: blog authors (Ideation). No technical setup needed — just a browser.

## Log in

1. Go to **https://baclogistics.co.za/admin/** (until the domain moves, use
   https://ambitious-bush-084cda303.7.azurestaticapps.net/admin/).
2. Sign in with your work Microsoft account (`developer@baclogistics.co.za`).
   The first time, you'll need to open the invitation link Rourke sends you —
   after that, it's just the normal Microsoft sign-in.

## Write a post

1. Click **+ New post**.
2. Fill in the form — it works like the old Couch admin:
   - **Title** — the headline. The **Slug** (the URL) fills itself in from the
     title; only change it if you must.
   - **Folder** — pick a category (Road Freight, Customs Clearing, Mining
     Transport, Liquor Transport), or leave "— none —" for a general post.
   - **Date** — defaults to today; controls where the post sorts on the blog.
   - **Featured image** — click *Upload new image* and pick a file (PNG/JPG/WebP,
     under 5 MB). Always fill in the **Alt text** (describe the image in one
     sentence — it matters for accessibility and Google).
   - **Body** — write the article. The toolbar has headings, bold/italic,
     lists, links and inline images.
   - **Tags / SEO fields** — same as Couch; leave blank anything you don't use.
3. Click **Save & publish**.

## That's it

The post is **live on the website within a minute or two** — usually seconds.
No deploys, no waiting on anyone. Check it at `/blog/`.

## Fixing mistakes

- **Edit**: open the post from the list, change it, Save — live again in
  moments. This works for all the old posts too.
- **Rename the URL**: change the Slug and Save — the post moves to the new
  URL and the old one is removed automatically.
- **Hide**: tick **Unpublished** and Save. The post disappears from the site
  but stays in your list (shown greyed out) for later.
- **Delete**: open the post → **Delete post**. Old versions are kept in
  storage for 30 days, so nothing is ever truly lost — ask Rourke if you need
  something restored.

## Good to know

- The blog list at `/blog/` shows 12 posts per page, newest first by date.
- Field lengths are capped (e.g. titles at 300 characters) — if Save reports
  an error, the message says exactly which field to shorten.
- Images you upload get a permanent address under `/blog/media/…`; old post
  images under `/couch/uploads/…` keep working as they always have.
